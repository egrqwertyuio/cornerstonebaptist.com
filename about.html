Our Purpose

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through faithful biblical teaching, genuine fellowship, and loving service to one another and our community.

Biblical Teaching

We are committed to the proclamation and teaching of God's Word as our final authority for faith and practice.

Genuine Fellowship

We cultivate authentic Christian community where believers encourage and support one another in love.

Loving Service

We serve one another and our community, demonstrating the love of Christ through practical acts of kindness.

Statement of Faith

What We Believe

1

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant, and authoritative Word of God, our final authority for all matters of faith and practice.

2

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, equal in power and glory.

3

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God who became man, lived a sinless life, died for our sins, and rose from the dead.

4

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone through faith alone in Christ alone, not by works, but resulting in a transformed life.

5

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and mission in the world.

6

Christ's Return

We believe in the personal, visible return of Jesus Christ to judge the living and the dead and to establish His eternal kingdom.

Our Leader

Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Dave Turner

Senior Pastor

Pastor Dave Turner has been faithfully serving Cornerstone Baptist Church, leading our congregation with biblical wisdom and genuine care for God's people.

With a heart for teaching God's Word and shepherding His flock, Pastor Dave is committed to equipping believers for ministry and helping each person grow in their relationship with Christ.

His preaching is characterized by clear exposition of Scripture, practical application, and a deep love for the Gospel. Pastor Dave and his family are dedicated to serving Christ and building up the body of believers at Cornerstone.

"My greatest joy is seeing lives transformed by the power of the Gospel and watching believers grow in their faith and love for Christ."

- Pastor Dave Turner
Our Story

Church History

Cornerstone Baptist Church has been a beacon of faith in Butler, Kentucky, serving our community with the unchanging message of the Gospel. Our name reflects our foundation - built on Jesus Christ, the solid rock and cornerstone of our faith.

Throughout our history, we have remained committed to faithful biblical teaching, warm Christian fellowship, and active service to our community. We continue to grow and adapt in our methods while staying true to the timeless truths of God's Word.

Today, Cornerstone Baptist Church is a vibrant community of believers united in our love for Christ and our desire to see lives transformed by the Gospel. We invite you to become part of our church family and join us in building lives on the solid rock of Christ.

Planning Your Visit

First Time Here?

What Time?

We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes early to find parking and get settled before the service begins.

What to Wear?

Come as you are! We have people dressed in everything from jeans to suits. The most important thing is that you're here.

What to Expect?

You'll experience warm hospitality, biblical teaching, and genuine worship. We'd love to meet you after the service!

What About Kids?

Children are always welcome in our services, and we provide age-appropriate programs and activities for them.

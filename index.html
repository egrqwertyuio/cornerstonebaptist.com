A Message from Pastor Dave Turner

Welcome to Cornerstone Baptist Church! We are so glad you're here. Whether you're seeking a church home or just exploring, we invite you to experience the love of Christ in our community.

Our church is built on the solid foundation of God's Word, and we are committed to faithful biblical teaching, genuine fellowship, and loving service to one another and our community.

We would love to meet you in person. Come join us this Sunday!

Serving Christ Through Various Ministries

Home Ministries

At Cornerstone Baptist Church, we believe in serving God and our community through diverse ministries. From nurturing the next generation to reaching out to those in need, each ministry reflects our commitment to Christ's love and the Great Commission.

For Teens & Young Adults

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry provides a vibrant and welcoming space for teenagers and young adults to grow in their faith, build meaningful friendships, and discover God's purpose for their lives.

Bible studies and devotions
Fellowship activities and events
Service opportunities
Mentorship and discipleship

Get Involved

Join us and be part of a community where young people can ask questions, grow in faith, and make lasting friendships.

Summer Program

Vacation Bible School

Our annual Vacation Bible School is a highlight of the summer! VBS provides children with an exciting week of Bible stories, worship, crafts, games, and activities that help them learn about Jesus in a fun and engaging way.

Engaging Bible lessons
Creative crafts and activities
Music and worship
Snacks and recreation

Join the Fun!

VBS is open to children of all ages. Check our Events page for dates and registration information for our next VBS.

Global Outreach

Operation Christmas Child

Through Operation Christmas Child, we have the privilege of sharing God's love with children around the world by packing shoeboxes filled with gifts, toys, and essential items.

Annual shoebox packing events
Reaching children worldwide
Sharing the Gospel through gifts
Community involvement opportunities

Make a Difference

This ministry allows us to be part of God's work in spreading the Gospel and showing love to children who may have never received a gift.

Reaching the Incarcerated

Prison Ministry

Our Prison Ministry is dedicated to bringing the hope of the Gospel to those who are incarcerated. We believe that no one is beyond the reach of God's grace and redemption.

Regular prison visits
Bible studies and worship
One-on-one discipleship
Providing hope and encouragement

Hope Behind Bars

Through prayer, Bible study, and personal connection, we share the life-changing message of Jesus Christ with those who need it most.
